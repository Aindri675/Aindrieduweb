Co-Founder

Albert Aindri

Albert Aindri is the visionary co-founder of Aindri EduWeb. With a strong passion for technology and education, Albert has played a crucial role in shaping the mission and strategy of the company. His leadership and innovative thinking have empowered Aindri EduWeb to become a leader in digital transformation, education, and innovation.

Albert brings years of experience in digital product development, strategic planning, and team building. He continues to inspire the company toward inclusive and impactful innovation, ensuring that Aindri EduWeb serves communities and organizations around the globe.

Albert is currently pursuing a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education at the prestigious Sogeri National School of Excellence, a top-tier institution committed to academic excellence and leadership development in Papua New Guinea.

His academic journey at Sogeri is marked by a deep curiosity for science and innovation, which directly fuels his drive to build practical digital solutions through Aindri EduWeb. He sees education not only as a personal pursuit but as a tool for social transformation.

At school, Albert actively participates in technology and science clubs, collaborating with peers on research projects and digital innovations that aim to address local community challenges through STEM-based solutions.

He believes that STEM education is the cornerstone of the future and aims to use his knowledge to bridge gaps in access to quality education and technology across underrepresented communities.
